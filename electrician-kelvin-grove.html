Frequently Asked Questions

Does Scholz have any insurance?

All of Scholz’s services are covered by Public liability indemnity insurance.

What the insurance covers in the event of an accident or unforeseen damage?

In the event of an accident or unforeseen damage as a result of our work, a third party, either our client or any other member of the general public, will be financially compensated.

Is your work guaranteed?

Scholz Electrical Offers a 12-month guarantee on workmanship on top of the product’s own warranty.

Do you provide free quotes?

Before committing to any electrical work for your residence, it is strongly recommended that you request a quote. Fortunately, we are happy to provide one at no cost! Please contact us today via phone or through our forms below – and start taking advantage of what we have to offer right away!

Do you charge a call-out fee?

Yes, Scholz does charge a call-out fee – and for a good reason. This remuneration compensates the electrician for their journey to your home by covering associated costs such as vehicle operation, insurance, and also time spent travelling both ways.
